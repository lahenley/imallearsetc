About

Hi! My name is Amanda Scott and I am the owner of I'm All Ears & ETC (Everything That's Cute). I started my small business about a year ago. I make Mickey Ears headbands, stretchie headbands, scrunchies, bags, light up ears, ear holders, and so much more! I have over 100 different fabrics you can choose from. Even though I specialize in making custom ears for your Disney trip, I have more than just Disney themed fabrics.
